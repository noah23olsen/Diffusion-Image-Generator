Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

AI Image Generator is a cutting-edge platform that uses artificial intelligence to create unique images from text descriptions. Our system employs advanced stable diffusion models to transform your written prompts into high-quality visual artwork in seconds.

The process involves several steps: 1) Your text prompt is analyzed by our AI, 2) The AI creates a mathematical representation of your description, 3) This representation is used to guide the image generation process through multiple refinement steps, 4) The final image is rendered and optimized for quality.

Usage & Features

For best results: 1) Be specific about style, medium, and subject matter, 2) Include artistic references (e.g., "in the style of Van Gogh"), 3) Specify lighting and composition details, 4) Use descriptive adjectives, 5) Keep prompts clear and concise while including important details.

We currently support generation of images in PNG and JPEG formats. Our standard output size is 512x512 pixels, optimized for both quality and generation speed. All generated images can be downloaded immediately after creation.

Technical Questions

Our platform uses state-of-the-art stable diffusion models, specifically optimized for creative image generation. These models have been trained on diverse datasets to understand and generate a wide range of artistic styles and subjects.

Our web-based platform works on any modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge) and doesn't require any downloads or installations. A stable internet connection is recommended for optimal performance.
