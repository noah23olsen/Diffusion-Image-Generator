Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI Image Generator?

AI Image Generator is a tool that uses artificial intelligence to create unique images from text descriptions. Simply enter a prompt, and our AI will generate a custom image based on your description.

How does it work?

Our system uses advanced diffusion models to understand your text prompt and generate corresponding images. The AI analyzes your description and creates a unique visual representation.

How do I write good prompts?

Good prompts are specific and descriptive. Include details about the subject, style, mood, and any specific elements you want in the image. Check our tutorials for more tips.

Can I use the generated images commercially?

Yes, you can use the generated images for both personal and commercial purposes. However, please review our terms of service for specific guidelines.

What image formats are supported?

We support PNG and JPG formats. Images are generated at 512x512 resolution by default.

How long does it take to generate an image?

Generation time varies but typically takes between 10-30 seconds depending on server load and prompt complexity.
